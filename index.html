Welcome to Good Deed!

Good Deed is a social media platform for sharing all the good in the world.

Anyone can join and post "Deeds" they have done or seen for everyone else to see, tagging other users that either did or recieved the deed.

Join us in sharing the good people can do!

In the future we plan on providing a proper web-based interface as well. Stay tuned!
